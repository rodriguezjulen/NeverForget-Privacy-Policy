Never Forget respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

## Data Storage
- All tasks, habits, and completion history are stored **locally on your device**.
- Data may also be stored in **iCloud / App Group storage** to enable synchronization between the app and its widgets.
- Never Forget does **not** use external servers to store your task data.

## Personal Information
- Never Forget does **not collect, track, or store personally identifiable information**.
- No account creation is required to use the app.

## Purchases and Subscriptions
- In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by the **Apple App Store**.
- Never Forget uses **RevenueCat** to manage subscription status.
- Only **anonymous purchase and entitlement information** is accessed to determine Premium feature availability.
- Never Forget does not have access to your payment details.

## Data Deletion
- You can delete all your data at any time by removing your tasks from the app.
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
